Total vaccinations per hundred vs. total covid cases per million

Try hovering over some countries and you should see the total vaccinations per hundred, along with the total covid cases per million:

  • We can see in the display above that there existed a wide distribution of vaccination rates and also occurences of covid-19 across countries globally.
  • One point to note is that locales with a larger population size (e.g. India, South America) tended to cluster around the same higher-range of number of vaccines received and a lower-range of number of cases of covid reported.
  • This higher vaccination rate to the number of cases reported in these high population countries may be due to population size skewing results in favor of vaccine distribution versus the actual number of cases of covid tracked.
