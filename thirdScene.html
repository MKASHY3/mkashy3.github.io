A global impact

Try hovering over some countries and you should see the country's name, along with their total number of covid cases per million people

Some countries are coloured black as data was unavailable for them.


  • We can examine the global impact of coronavirus here. When you hover over a country you will see the total number of cases of coronavirus reported per million people.
  • At first glance, there were no clear trends between socioeconomic status of a country and the number of cases reported, which suggests that there may potentially be innacuracies in reporting.
  • Perhaps countries with lower socioeconomic status had fewer resources to accurately track and report the total number of cases of coronavirus to inflict citizens of their country. This may explain why the number of cases of corona in the United States, for example, was about 300K per million, while India, a country with a much larger population size, had solely 32K cases reported per million.
