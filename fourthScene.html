Total vaccinations per hundred vs. People vaccinated per hundred in the United States

Try selecting some American states from the dropdown filter to see how their vaccination rates (total and by people) changed from December 2020 to August 2021.

  • Finally, an opportunity for you-the viewer-to interact with the data!
  • Here, we can select from the dropdown menu at the top and filter by states located within the United States of America.
  • Each state has associated data displayed above regarding the total number of vaccinations for covid-19 received per hundred people.
  • On the vertical (y-axis) we see the total number of vaccinations distributed per hundred and on the horizontal (x-axis) we see the total number of people vaccinated per hundred.
